About our company

SST Group Holding PTY LTD was initiated out of the need to offer reliable and satisfactory services that will help our clients meet their business end goals. SST is an abbreviation of Servitude Service Trading, Servitude meaning the state of being a slave or completely subject to someone.

Hence our motto, “We lose ourselves to Discover and Cover your needs”. We take pride in operating from honesty and integrity as these values form the fundamental pillars of our operation.

SST Group Holding is a 100% black owned company and level 1 BBEE number contributor company and offers the following services:

  1. (1) Accounting & Taxation Services,
  2. (2) Audit & Advisory Services
  3. (3) ICT & Other Supply Services and
  4. (4) Consultancy Services. These are offered through the company's subsidiaries namely,
    5. SST ACCTAX SERVICEES (PTY) LTD, SST AUDIT & ADVISORY SERVICES (PTY) LTD. SST ICT AND SUPPLY (PTY) LTD, SST CONSULTANCY SERVICES (PTY) LTD as well as SST FOUNDATION
